About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the community through various charitable initiatives. We provide support and resources to those in need.

Our Initiatives

👩‍🎓

Women Empowerment

Raising awareness among women about the Prime Minister's Self-Employment Scheme and implementing the Sukanya Scheme.

👧👦

Child Welfare

Taking care of orphaned children, assisting in their marriage, and running an orphanage.

👵🏽👴🏽

Elder Care

Operating an old age home to provide shelter and care for senior citizens.

⚖️

Social Justice

Raising a voice against oppression, corruption, and ensuring justice for all.

🚺

Women's Safety

Preventing crimes against women and supporting victims.

🏥

Healthcare

Establishing free hospitals for the poor and underprivileged.

💒

Community Support

Organizing mass weddings for poor women and daughters.

📚

Education

Emphasizing the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (Universal Education Campaign).

🧹

Clean India

Making India a clean and addiction-free nation through awareness campaigns.
